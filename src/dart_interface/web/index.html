Welcome to the user study!

The study is in a form of an opinion questionnaire.

Choose a topic from the drop down menu.
You will be presented with a pair of opinions from Twitter. You are asked to choose the message you agree with the most by clicking on it.
To aid your decision, context tweets appear under the message if you hover over it.
After selecting your choice, you will be presented with the next pair of arguments to choose from. The quiz continues in this way through nine pairs of arguments and ends with a results screen. Following the experiment, there will be a discussion about your experience.

Messages displayed are taken from Twitter.com and can be found by performing the same queries on their website. Opinions expressed in the messages displayed do not reflect our opinions and may contain upsetting content.
